About Sam Fedel Farms

Sam Fedel Farms is dedicated to providing sustainable farming solutions, innovative poultry care, and modern fishery services for the community. Our mission is to combine traditional expertise with modern technology to cultivate excellence in every project.

Business Owner: Felicia Eke – a passionate entrepreneur committed to sustainable agriculture, quality poultry production, and healthy fishery practices. She ensures that every aspect of the farm reflects professionalism, innovation, and community care.
